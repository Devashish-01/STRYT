# STRYT — Community Guidelines

**Effective Date:** [To be set by STRYT on publication]
**Last Updated:** 19 July 2026
**Version:** 1.0 (draft for legal review)

STRYT is your street, and your people. It works because real neighbours — customers, shopkeepers, and independent providers — treat each other with honesty and respect. These Community Guidelines explain how to behave across the **community feed, stories, reviews, Q&A, messages, requests, and listings**. They sit alongside the stricter, enforceable [Acceptable Use Policy](acceptable-use-policy.md) and form part of the [Terms & Conditions](terms-and-conditions.md). Where these Guidelines and the AUP overlap, the AUP governs enforcement.

---

## 1. The spirit

- **Be a good neighbour.** Be helpful, honest, and kind. Assume good faith.
- **Keep it local and useful.** Post things that genuinely help people around you — recommendations, alerts, giveaways, lost-and-found, honest reviews, real offers.
- **Be real.** Be who you say you are. Don't pretend to be someone else or run fake profiles.

---

## 2. Do

- Share genuine recommendations, questions, and neighbourhood updates.
- Leave **honest reviews and ratings** based on real experiences; a "Verified booking" tag shows a review tied to a real booking.
- Give and receive **vouches and endorsements** truthfully.
- Post clear, accurate listings, prices, and availability if you sell.
- Report content or people that break the rules, using the in-app **Report** option.
- Respect people's privacy and their choice to keep their real name, contact, and location private.
- Use the safety tools (live location, emergency contacts) responsibly to look after yourself and others.

---

## 3. Don't

- **No lies or scams.** No fake listings, fake reviews, fake availability, fake "I paid" claims, misleading prices, or fraud of any kind.
- **No harassment or hate.** No bullying, threats, stalking, or hateful/discriminatory content targeting anyone based on religion, caste, gender, sexual orientation, disability, ethnicity, or similar.
- **No harmful or illegal content or trade.** Nothing unlawful; no weapons, drugs, counterfeit or stolen goods, sexual services, or anything you're not licensed to sell (see the AUP for the full list).
- **No sexual, obscene, or violent content**, and absolutely nothing that endangers, sexualises, or exploits children.
- **No privacy violations.** Don't share someone's personal information, photos, or location without their consent (no doxxing). Don't screenshot and spread private chats to harm someone.
- **No spam or manipulation.** Don't flood feeds, DMs, reachouts, or reviews with unsolicited promotion; don't rig ratings, leaderboards, group-buys, or search.
- **No impersonation.** Don't pose as another person, business, provider, or as STRYT staff.
- **No misusing safety features** to track, harass, or endanger anyone.

---

## 4. Reviews, ratings, and reputation — fair play

- Review only genuine experiences. Don't review your own business/provider profile, and don't post or buy fake or retaliatory reviews.
- Sellers may reply to reviews publicly and professionally, and may report a review — but must not threaten reviewers or trade perks for changing honest feedback.
- Ratings, vouches, endorsements, badges, achievements, and leaderboards reflect real activity. Gaming them is not allowed and STRYT may reset or remove manipulated reputation.

---

## 5. Selling and promoting

- If you run a shop or provide a service, keep listings, hours, stock, and prices accurate, and honour what customers book.
- You can post to the feed as your business/provider identity — keep it relevant and non-spammy.
- Follow the [Merchant & Provider Terms](merchant-terms.md), including your responsibility for taxes, licences, quality, and refunds.

---

## 6. Meeting in person

STRYT often leads to real-world meetings and payments. Meet in public where you can, verify who you're dealing with and the payment details, and tell someone where you're going. A **STRYT Verified** badge is a helpful signal but not a safety guarantee. Look after yourself and others.

---

## 7. Enforcement

Breaking these Guidelines (and the AUP) can lead to content being removed, features limited, reputation reset, or accounts suspended or terminated — with reporting to authorities for serious or illegal conduct. We aim to be fair and proportionate, and Sellers can appeal a suspension in-app. Content grievances go through the [Grievance Redressal Policy](grievance-redressal-policy.md).

We don't remove lawful content just because it's critical or unflattering — honest, respectful criticism is welcome.

---

## 8. Reporting

See something that breaks the rules? Use the **Report** option on the profile, listing, request, post, or review, or **Account → Help & Support**. For formal grievances, see the [Grievance Redressal Policy](grievance-redressal-policy.md).

Thanks for helping keep STRYT a place neighbours can trust.
