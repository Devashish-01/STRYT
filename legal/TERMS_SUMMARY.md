## What STRYT is

STRYT helps you discover shops and independent service providers near you, ask your neighbourhood for things you need, get quotes, book appointments, join walk-in queues from your phone, chat, and keep up with a local community feed — all focused on your own area.

**STRYT is a platform that connects people. We are not the shop, the worker, or the bank.** The actual buying, selling, and paying happens directly between you and the other person.

---

## The big things to know

- **You need to be 18 or older** to sign up and use STRYT. It's built for use in India.
- **You sign in with Google.** Keep that Google account secure — anyone with it can get into your STRYT account.
- **One account, many roles.** The same account can be a customer, run one or more shops, and offer services as a solo provider. You switch between these "hats" yourself. You can set an optional PIN to protect switching into a shop/provider view on a shared phone.

## Money: how payments really work

This is the most important thing to understand:

- **STRYT never holds your money.** We are not a bank, wallet, or payment gateway.
- You pay the shop or provider **directly** — by UPI (we just create the QR/link) or by **cash** in person.
- After paying, you tap **"I have paid"**, and the other side **confirms** or **rejects** it. These are just **status labels for your convenience** — they are **not** proof of payment and not a receipt from STRYT.
- Words like "held" or "release" in a deal are just labels inside the app. **We are not holding any money at any point.**
- **Because we never hold your money, we can't give refunds.** Any refund is between you and the shop/provider. Pay carefully, check you're paying the right person, and confirm only after you actually get what you paid for.
- Paid STRYT features (like "Pro" plans, lead packs, or "boosts") are **not on sale in the app right now** — there's no payment gateway wired up.

## Booking, queues, and deals

- **Estimates and wait times are guesses**, not promises. A shop can accept or decline your booking, and control its own queue.
- Accepting a quote creates a **Deal** with a real neighbour — treat it as a real commitment. Cancelling or not showing up can affect your reputation on STRYT.
- Cancellations follow the [Refund & Cancellation Policy](refund-cancellation-policy.md). The shop/provider sets its own practical rules too.

## Safety

- STRYT has safety tools: live-location sharing with your chosen emergency contacts, one-time location requests (the other person must approve), and shareable tracking links.
- **Never misuse these to track, harass, or endanger anyone.** You meet people from the app at your own risk — use the same caution you would with any stranger.
- A **"STRYT Verified"** badge means a human at STRYT reviewed some documents once. It is a helpful signal, **not a guarantee** about that person or business.

## Your privacy (quick version)

- **Your real name is private by default** — strangers see your public handle (alias). You choose what else is public.
- **We don't store your location history — only your last known spot.** Live-location sharing is temporary and you can stop it anytime.
- We collect what we need to run the app (profile, location, listings, bookings, payments-as-recorded, messages, and — only if you choose verification — ID documents).
- We use OpenStreetMap for maps, Firebase for Google sign-in and push, Supabase to store data, and Vercel for hosting/anonymous analytics. **We don't sell your data.**
- The full details, your rights under India's Data Protection law (DPDP Act), and how to exercise them are in the [Privacy Policy](privacy-policy.md).

## The "smart price" feature isn't a chatbot

When you post a request, STRYT can suggest a price. That's just **an average of recent similar quotes nearby** — not an AI assistant, and it doesn't read your text or send it to any AI service. Treat suggestions as rough guidance only.

## Leaving STRYT

- You can ask to delete your account in **Settings**. There's a **30-day grace period** where it's hidden but you can undo it with one tap. After that, we permanently remove or anonymise it and delete your uploaded files (including any ID documents).
- You'll need to wrap up any **active deals** and open payment statuses first.
