# Reviews, Vouches & Trust

STRYT's reputation system helps you tell a real, good experience from an unverifiable claim.

**Reviews.** Leave a star rating and comment on any shop or provider. If it's tied to a real, completed booking, it's automatically marked with a **"Verified booking"** badge — a strong signal that the review is genuine.

**Vouches & Endorsements (providers).** Separate from star ratings, any neighbour can give a provider a one-tap **"I vouch for this person"**, or **endorse** them for a specific listed skill — both shown as simple counts and avatar stacks on their profile. Useful for saying "I trust this person" even before you've formally booked them.

**Leaderboard.** A monthly, local ranking of the top providers and most helpful neighbours in your area — check it to see who's active and trusted nearby.

**Achievements.** Personal badges for milestones — your first request, your first completed Deal, helping 5 neighbours, a 5-star rating, and more. Find these on your **Achievements** screen from your Profile.

**STRYT Verified badge.** This badge is only ever granted after a real member of the STRYT team manually reviews documents a business or provider submits — never automatic. Treat it as a meaningful trust signal, though not a guarantee of every future interaction.

**Report a listing or a review.** If something looks like spam, a scam, or is fake/offensive/miscategorized, use the Report option on the profile or review — reports are anonymous and go straight to the moderation team.
