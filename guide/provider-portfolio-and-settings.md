# Portfolio, Settings & Signing Out

**Portfolio** (Profile Hub → Portfolio). A captioned gallery of your past work — visual proof of quality, which matters even more for a solo provider without a storefront to walk past.

**Provider Settings.** Control your profile visibility, contact-detail privacy, and local notification preferences. Note: your **service radius** is *not* set here — it's read-only in Settings with a link to Profile → Identity, where the actual control lives.

**Signing out.** Your Profile Hub has a direct red **Log out** button at the bottom — no need to switch back to your personal Customer view first.

**What providers don't have (by design).** Provider accounts are intentionally a lighter, solo-operator toolkit — unlike businesses, there's no live walk-in queue, no public Q&A board, no public review replies, and no delegated/staff login. These are shop-specific tools that don't apply to a one-person operation.
