# Tracking a Deal — Live Status & Location

Once a Deal is confirmed, you don't have to guess what's happening.

**Live job status.** The person helping you can mark themselves **Leaving now**, **On the way**, **Arrived**, or **Working** — you see this update live on the Deal screen.

**Live location tracking link.** You can generate a shareable link so someone else — a family member, a friend — can watch the responder's live location en route, in real time, with no STRYT account required on their end. Handy if you want someone else to know when help is arriving.

**Payment on a Deal.** Payment happens directly between you and the other person — by UPI (STRYT generates the QR/link) or cash — and is tracked with the same "claim & confirm" system used everywhere else in the app. See **"Paying Safely on STRYT"** for exactly how that works and why STRYT never touches your money.

**Confirmation window.** Both sides generally need to confirm key steps within a set window (shown on-screen) — if it lapses without confirmation, the Deal's status reflects that, and you can usually pick the conversation back up rather than starting over.

**Disputes.** If the job goes sideways, raise a dispute directly from the Deal screen with a written reason — STRYT's team reviews it as a neutral third party.
