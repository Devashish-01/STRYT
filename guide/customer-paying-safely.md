# Paying Safely on STRYT

**The most important thing to understand: STRYT never holds, processes, or touches your money.** It's not a bank, wallet, or payment gateway. Every payment — for an appointment, a Deal, a queue visit, or a walk-in purchase — happens **directly between you and the seller**.

**How it works — Claim & Confirm:**
1. You pay the seller directly, either by scanning/tapping a **UPI QR code or deep-link** STRYT generates (works with GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, any UPI app), or by handing over **cash**.
2. You tap **"I have paid."**
3. The seller must then explicitly **confirm** they received it — or **reject** the claim if they can't verify it.

**Why this matters to you.** This "claim & confirm" flow is a shared record for both sides' benefit — it is *not* a receipt from STRYT, and STRYT cannot verify the underlying UPI transfer. **Only tap "I have paid" once you've actually sent the money**, and **only confirm receipt (as a seller) once you've actually checked your own bank/UPI app.**

**Paying as a walk-in.** Standing in a shop with no prior booking? Open its profile, pick catalog items and quantities, and pay directly — no appointment or queue entry needed first.

**If something's wrong.** Because STRYT never holds the money, it can't issue refunds directly — any refund is between you and the seller. If you're stuck, use **Account → Help & Support**, or raise a **dispute** if it's tied to a Deal.
