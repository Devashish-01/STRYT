# Privacy & Your Profile

**Your real name is private by default.** Strangers see your **username/alias**, not your real name, unless you specifically turn on "Real name" visibility.

**What you control** (Profile → Edit, and Settings): your display name, public @handle, photo, neighbourhood, and individual privacy switches for your real name, posts, requests, badges, phone, email, city, rating, and exact location. Most of these default to **off** (private) — STRYT doesn't assume you want to be fully public.

**Notification & discovery settings** (Settings): your discovery/alert radius, quiet hours, which notification types you receive, what shows on your public profile, and your app language (English, Hindi, or Marathi).

**Saved, following & lists.** Bookmark shops/providers/requests for later, follow people/shops for updates, or organise your saves into your own named lists (e.g. "Favourite restaurants") — find these under Bookmarks and Lists.

**My Activity.** A personal archive of everything you've posted — Stories (plus saved Highlights) and Community posts — all in one place, including your business/provider activity if you act as one of those too.

**Deleting your account.** Submit a reason in Settings to request deletion. Your account enters a **30-day grace period** — hidden from others, but recoverable with one tap — before it's permanently and irreversibly removed. Note: you'll need to resolve any active Deals first.
