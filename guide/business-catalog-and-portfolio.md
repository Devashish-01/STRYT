# Catalog, Portfolio & Hours

**Catalog / Menu** (Store → Catalog). Add products or services with photos and a price (plus an optional sale price). Choose a stock mode per item:
- **Always available** — unlimited, like a haircut or a repair visit.
- **Limited stock** — a countable quantity that automatically sells out and can be restocked.

Works equally well for a restaurant menu or a shop's limited-inventory products.

**Portfolio** (Store → Portfolio). A captioned photo gallery of completed work or finished projects — separate from your sellable catalog. Use it to show quality, not just what's for sale.

**Hours & Slots** (Store → Hours). Set your weekly working hours (or 24×7), an instant "open right now" override for today, the length of each bookable appointment slot, and notes for holidays/special dates. This drives every booking slot customers see and keeps your public open/closed status accurate — keep it current.
