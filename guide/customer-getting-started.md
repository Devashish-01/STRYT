# Getting Started on STRYT

**Signing in.** Tap "Continue with Google" on the welcome screen — one tap creates your account or signs you back in. No separate password.

**Looking around first.** Not ready to sign in? Tap "Look around first" to browse shops, providers, requests, and the community feed within 1 km of your device, no account needed. Anything that needs an account (booking, messaging, joining a queue, posting) shows a "sign in to continue" prompt instead of a broken button.

**Your first-time setup.** The first time you sign in, STRYT asks for:
- Your **name**
- A **unique username** (your public handle — this is what strangers see, not your real name, so pick something you're comfortable with; it must be 3–20 characters, letters/numbers/`.`/`_` only)
- Your **neighbourhood/area** (optional but recommended — this is what makes results actually near you)
- A **profile photo** or one of 10 emoji avatars (optional)
- Your **phone number** and preferred **language** (optional)
- How far you'd like alerts and discovery to reach (optional, adjustable later)

Name and username are required — everything else can be filled in later from your profile.

**Location permission.** Right after setup, STRYT asks once for location access so it can show what's genuinely nearby. If you say no, it won't ask again, and it's upfront that results will be less relevant without it. You can always set your area manually instead.

**Your privacy from day one.** Your real name is private by default — other users see your username/alias, not your name, unless you choose to make it public in Settings.
