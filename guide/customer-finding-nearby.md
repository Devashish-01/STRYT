# Finding Shops, Providers & Things Nearby

**Home** gives you a personalised snapshot: a live weather-and-time-of-day header, your active queue position or upcoming appointment if you have one, a stories bar, quick category shortcuts, and rails of nearby shops and providers.

**Explore** is the main browsing screen — every shop ("Shops") and independent provider ("Helpers") near you, filterable by category with an adjustable search radius.

**Search** gives you fast type-ahead results across shops, providers, and categories, keeps your recent searches, and suggests what's trending nearby. Tap the bell icon on a search to save it as an **alert** — STRYT notifies you the moment a matching new listing joins your area.

**Categories** — browse the full category grid (with live counts of nearby shops/providers in each) when you know the type of help you need but not a specific name.

**Map** is a visual way to browse: shops, providers, open requests, and stories all show as pins, with a search-radius ring and an "⚡ Available now" filter for providers free right now. You can drop a pin or long-press to set your location manually, and a special **World** mode removes the distance filter entirely to show the newest listings everywhere.

**Guests** can use all of the above, capped to 1 km around their device — sign in for unlimited range.
