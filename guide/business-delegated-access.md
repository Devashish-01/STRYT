# Delegated Access & Your Team

**Granting access.** From **Account Settings → Business access**, instantly grant another STRYT user (identified by their phone, email, or @handle) full management access to your business — no separate login for them to set up. They manage it from their own phone, their own account. Access lasts until you revoke it.

**From the Business hub:** the "Delegated access" row jumps straight to this same screen.

**Revoking access.** See everyone with access to your business, revoke it instantly, and review a history of past grants — all from the same Business access screen.

**Team roster.** A simple internal list of staff/managers by name, phone, and role, kept for your own reference under Business → Settings → Team. This is just a record — it's separate from who actually has app login access (that's Delegated Access above).
