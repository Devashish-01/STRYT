# Business Settings & Signing Out

**Business → Settings** is your central control panel:
- Whether your shop is **publicly visible** at all
- **Local notification preferences**
- **Contact-detail privacy** — what's shown to customers
- Your basic **staff roster** (see "Delegated Access & Your Team")
- A **"temporarily close shop"** switch for when you're away

**Signing out.** The Business hub has a direct red **Log out** button at the bottom — you don't need to switch back to your personal Customer view first just to sign out of STRYT entirely.

**Switch PIN.** If you share a device (like a shop counter phone), set an optional PIN in Settings → Security that's required to switch **into** this business's console — protecting it from being casually opened by whoever picks up the phone.
