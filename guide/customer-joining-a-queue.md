# Joining a Live Queue Remotely

If a shop is running a live walk-in queue, you don't have to stand in line to hold your place.

**How it works:**
1. Open the shop's profile page and join the queue, picking your **party size**.
2. You'll see your **position**, how many people are ahead of you, and a **live estimated wait time**.
3. Go run other errands nearby — you don't need to be physically present until it's close to your turn.

**When it's your turn.** Your status flips to **"Called"** with a prominent "head in now" alert — both on the shop's page and as a live card on your Home screen, so you won't miss it.

**Leaving a queue.** You can leave instantly while you're just waiting. Once you've been called or are already being served, leaving requires a confirmation warning, since you'd lose your place — this is intentional, to stop accidental taps from costing you your spot.

**A note on wait times.** The estimate is calculated from the shop's own average-time-per-customer setting and updates as the queue moves — treat it as a helpful guide, not a guarantee.
