# Listing Your Business on STRYT

**Setting up takes about 4 steps:**
1. Name, category, and how far your shop should broadcast to nearby customers.
2. Your pinned location and address.
3. Up to 4 photos, plus an optional opening offer.
4. Contact details and your weekly hours.

Your shop goes **live immediately** after you submit — customers can find it right away — while STRYT's team runs a verification review in the background. You don't need to wait for that review to start operating.

**Multiple businesses.** One account can own more than one business — each gets its own full management console.

**Where to start:** "Add a business" from your Profile, or the Manage hub.

**After you're live:** head to your new shop's **Home** dashboard — see "The Business Home Dashboard" guide for what's on it and what to do first.
