# Payments — Setup, Claims & Confirming

**STRYT never holds your money.** Customers pay you **directly**, by UPI or cash — STRYT just provides the QR/link and a shared record of who claims to have paid.

**Setting up how you get paid** (Business → Settings): add a **UPI ID** (this auto-generates your payment QR) or upload your own QR image. You also choose your **payment timing policy** — whether customers must pay before you accept a booking, or can pay around the time of service.

**Confirming a payment claim.** When a customer taps "I have paid," you'll see the claim on your **Payments dashboard** (Business → Payments) or right on the booking/queue entry. **Confirm it only after checking your own bank/UPI app** — never on the strength of a screenshot alone. If you can't verify it, **reject** the claim instead.

**The Payments dashboard** is your single reconciliation screen: every claim waiting for confirmation, every outstanding unpaid booking or queue visit (with a one-tap **reminder** you can send), and a history of everything already confirmed paid.

**Walk-in payments.** A customer standing in your shop with no prior booking can pay you directly from your profile — pick catalog items and quantities and record it, or use "Pay now" if the customer initiates it from their side.
