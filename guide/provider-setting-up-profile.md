# Setting Up Your Provider Profile

Independent workers — plumbers, tutors, freelancers — can get discoverable and bookable without needing a shop at all.

**Setup takes about 4 steps:**
1. Your professional name and category (or propose a new category if nothing fits).
2. Location, bio, starting price, service radius, and availability.
3. Up to 5 portfolio photos.
4. A **required face photo** — this one isn't optional, since it's how customers know who's actually showing up.

Your profile goes **live immediately** after you submit. One STRYT account can hold exactly **one** provider profile (but can also own multiple businesses separately).

**Where to start:** "Become a provider" from your Profile, or the Manage hub.
