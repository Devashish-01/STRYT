# Services Catalog & Schedule

**Services catalog** (Profile Hub → Services). List fixed-price services with photos and an optional sale price, either **unlimited** or **countable-stock** availability — gives customers clear, upfront pricing before they even message you.

**Schedule & availability** (Profile Hub → Schedule). Set your recurring working days and hours, and the length of each bookable slot — with a live preview of exactly how it reads to customers, so you know what they'll see before you save it. Keeps bookings limited to times you're actually free.
