# Managing Bookings

The Bookings tab gives you full control over your schedule — including the walk-in and last-minute cases a purely online system would miss.

**What you can do:**
- **Accept or decline** appointment requests, with a note explaining why if you decline.
- **Cancel** a confirmed booking with a reason.
- **Mark no-shows** when a customer doesn't turn up.
- **Block off** individual slots or whole days — including **recurring blocks** (e.g. every Sunday).
- **Manually add a walk-in booking** for a customer served in person with no prior booking.
- See a day's bookings with a **shareable, WhatsApp-style summary** you can send to staff.

Payment on a booking follows the same claim/confirm system as the rest of the app — see "Payments — Setup, Claims & Confirming" for how that works from your side.
