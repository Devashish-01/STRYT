# Reviews, Q&A & Finding Local Work

**Replying to reviews** (Business → Reviews). Publicly respond to any customer review — a good way to address feedback in the open and build trust with future customers. You can also report a review to moderation if it's fake or abusive.

**Customer Q&A** (Business → Q&A, and inline on Home). Customers can publicly ask a question on your shop's page; you answer it once, and answered/upvoted questions surface for future visitors — saving you from answering the same question over and over in private messages.

**Customer reachouts** (Business → Customer reachouts). A log of every customer interaction that isn't a formal booking — calls, directions taps, story replies, coupon claims, and questions — each markable as handled. Surfaces real interest that would otherwise be invisible.

**Finding local job requests** (Business → Find requests). See open customer requests that match your shop's category and your broadcast radius, and send a quote to compete for the work — a second channel for winning customers beyond people finding your shop directly.
