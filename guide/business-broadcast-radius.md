# Setting Your Broadcast Radius

Your **broadcast radius** controls how far around your shop STRYT shows you to nearby customers — a wider radius reaches more people; a tighter one keeps you hyperlocal.

**Where to change it:** Business hub → **Broadcast radius**. Pick a preset distance or set a custom one, then Save.

**Where you'll see the current value:** your **Home** dashboard header shows it read-only (e.g. "📡 3 km reach") as a quick reminder — to actually change it, use the dedicated Broadcast radius screen from the Business hub, not the dashboard.

**What it affects:** how far your shop shows up in Explore/Search/Map results, and the radius used to match you with nearby job requests on the "Find requests" screen.
