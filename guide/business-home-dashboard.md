# The Business Home Dashboard

**Home** (the first tab in your business console) is your daily command centre — everything that needs a same-day response, in one glance.

**What's on it:**
- A **setup checklist** for anything you haven't finished yet (dismissible once you've seen it)
- An instant **"mark shop open now"** override
- A **live queue snapshot**, if you run one
- An **"Action needed"** worklist — pending bookings, payment claims waiting on you, unanswered customer questions
- **Today's bookings** at a glance
- A **payments summary** — what's recorded, what's still waiting for confirmation
- **Matching nearby job leads** from the Requests system
- Quick actions to **post an update**, **post a story**, or **share your shop's QR code**
- Your current **broadcast radius**, shown read-only in the header — tap into "Business → Broadcast radius" to change it (see that guide)

**Bottom navigation:** Home, Queue, Bookings, Store, Business — the last one ("Business") is your hub for everything else: profile, payments, delegated access, verification, settings, and a direct **Log out** button.
