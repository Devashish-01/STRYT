# Money, Earnings & Your Service Radius

**Money tab.** See your total earnings, this week's earnings, jobs completed, and a full itemised earnings history. Confirm or reject payment claims here, and set up **how you get paid** — your UPI/QR details and payment timing policy.

**Confirming a payment claim.** When a customer taps "I have paid," check your own bank/UPI app before confirming — never confirm on a screenshot alone. If you can't verify it, reject the claim instead.

**Your service radius** — how far you're willing to travel or serve customers — is shown **read-only** on both your Today dashboard and in Settings. To actually change it, go to **Profile → Identity** (the Identity editor) and adjust it there — this is the one place service radius is set, so it's easy to find and there's no duplicate control to get out of sync.

**STRYT never holds your money** — every payment is direct between you and the customer, by UPI or cash. See the customer-side "Paying Safely on STRYT" guide for the full picture of how claim & confirm works.
