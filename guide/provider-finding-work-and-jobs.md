# Finding Work & Managing Jobs

**Find work** (Find work tab). Browse open customer requests matching your category and area, respond with a quote — you can save **reusable quote templates** so you're not typing the same pitch every time — and track sent quotes and negotiate directly with the requester.

**Jobs** (Jobs tab — your booking calendar). Accept or decline requests, cancel with a reason, mark no-shows, block time off, add a walk-in booking, and see a day's schedule with a shareable summary. The same day-to-day booking control a shop owner gets, sized for a solo operator.

**Reachouts** (Profile Hub → Reachouts). Calls and messages from customers that aren't formal bookings — worth checking so nothing falls through the cracks.
