# Managing Your Live Queue

The Queue tab turns a physical waiting line into something you can run from anywhere in the shop.

**What you control:**
- **Open or close** the queue whenever you want to take walk-ins remotely.
- Set the **average time per customer** — this is what drives everyone's live wait-time estimate, so keep it realistic.
- **Call** the next customer when you're ready for them.
- Mark someone **arrived** or **served**, or **remove a no-show**.
- **Confirm or reject payment claims** directly from the queue — no need to jump to a separate payments screen.

**Why customers like it.** They can join from their phone, see their position and people ahead, and only need to walk in once it's actually their turn — see the customer-side "Joining a Live Queue Remotely" guide for what they see.
