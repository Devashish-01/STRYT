# Switching Between Customer, Business & Provider

One STRYT account can be more than one thing — a shopper, a shop owner, *and* an independent service provider, all at once, without separate logins.

**Your "hats."** From your Profile, switch which identity you're currently using the app as — your personal Customer view, one of your businesses, or your provider profile — via the quick switcher. This switcher is also available throughout the business/provider management consoles for fast switching without going back to your profile first.

**Becoming a business owner or provider.** Tap "Add a business" or "Become a provider" from your Profile or the Manage hub. Either can be set up in a few minutes and goes live right away (with a verification review happening in the background). A person can own more than one business, but only ever **one** provider profile.

**Switch PIN.** You can set an optional 4–6 digit PIN that's required every time you switch **into** a business or provider console — but never required to switch back to your personal Customer view. This protects the business/provider dashboard on a shared device (like a shop counter phone) from being casually opened by anyone who picks it up. Set this in **Settings → Security** once you own a business or provider profile.

**Logging out of a console.** Both the Business hub and the Provider profile hub have a direct **Log out** button, so you don't need to switch back to your Customer view first just to sign out entirely.
