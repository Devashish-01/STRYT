# Notifications, Support & Your Account

**In-app notifications.** The notification bell (present throughout the app) is a running list of everything relevant to you — new offers, proposal replies, Deal updates, queue turns, appointment changes, community replies, and more — with unread counts and one tap to jump to the relevant screen.

**How alerts reach you.** STRYT delivers most notifications two ways at once: the in-app bell, and a push notification (in your browser on the website, or via the native app on Android). Control categories and quiet hours in **Settings**.

**Contacting support.** Use **Account → Help & Support** to submit a request by category (complaint, general inquiry, account issue, listing issue, or suggestion) with your email and a message — this goes directly to the STRYT team.

**Reporting a bug.** Use **Account → Report a bug** to describe an app problem, tagged with which "hat" (Customer/Business/Provider) you were using at the time.

**Terms & Privacy.** You'll be asked to review and accept STRYT's Terms & Conditions and Privacy Policy the first time you sign in (and again if they're materially updated) — you can re-read either any time from **Account → Legal & Policies**.

**Still stuck?** Check the rest of this Guide first (search above, or browse by topic) — most day-to-day questions are answered here faster than waiting on a support reply.
