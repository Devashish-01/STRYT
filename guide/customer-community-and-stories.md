# The Community Feed & Stories

**Community Feed.** A local, public feed for neighbourhood posts — Ask Neighbours (recommendations), Lost & Found, Alerts, Giveaways, Polls, and Shoutouts — with likes, threaded comments, and a "Trending nearby" sort. Everyone can browse, including guests; posting/liking/commenting/voting needs an account.

**Stories.** Short-lived photo posts (from 1 hour up to 7 days) that show in a bar at the top of the app and disappear after they expire. You can pin favourites permanently as **Highlights** so they don't disappear. Stories support emoji reactions, and you can see who's viewed yours.

**"Neighbourhood Today" pulse.** A live snapshot on Home of what's actually happening near you right now — active alerts, requests waiting for help, new shops that opened, and providers currently available — without you having to go looking for it.

**Posting as your business/provider.** If you run a shop or work as a provider, you can post to the Community feed under that identity instead of your personal one — a way to participate in the local conversation as your brand.
