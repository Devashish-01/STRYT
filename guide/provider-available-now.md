# Going Available Now

One tap makes you visible to nearby customers as available for **immediate work**, for a set window of time (default 3 hours) — captures same-day, urgent demand that your fixed schedule would otherwise miss.

**Where to toggle it:** the Today dashboard, or the Availability screen.

**What customers see:** an "⚡ Available now" filter on the Map screen surfaces providers who've switched this on, right now, near them.

This is separate from your regular **Schedule** (recurring working days/hours) — think of "Available now" as "I happen to be free for the next few hours," on top of your normal working pattern.
