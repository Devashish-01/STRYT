# The Provider Today Screen

**Today** (the first tab in your provider console) is built for someone checking their phone between jobs — not sitting at a desk.

**What's on it:**
- A live worklist of what needs a response **right now** — payment claims, pending bookings
- **Today's schedule**
- A **"Go available now"** toggle for picking up immediate jobs
- A **money snapshot**
- A nudge when open customer requests match your skills

**Bottom navigation:** Today, Jobs, Find work, Money, Profile — the last one is your identity hub, covering everything else (identity/service radius, services, schedule, portfolio, reachouts, verification, payments, settings, and a direct **Log out** button).

Your current **service radius** shows read-only in the Today header (e.g. "📡 10 km reach") — to change it, go to **Profile → Identity**, not Today itself.
