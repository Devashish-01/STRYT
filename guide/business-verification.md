# Getting Your STRYT Verified Badge

**How it works.** Upload supporting documents from Business → Verification. A real member of the STRYT team manually reviews them — this badge is **never** granted automatically. They'll either approve the "STRYT Verified" badge (shown publicly on your profile) or reject it with a written reason and a chance to resubmit.

**Why it's manual.** Keeping the review human-only is what protects the badge's credibility — customers can trust that "Verified" always means an actual person checked.

**If you're rejected.** Read the reason carefully, fix what's flagged, and resubmit — there's no limit on retries.

**If your shop gets suspended.** You can submit an **appeal** explaining your case; STRYT's team reviews it and responds with a note.
