# Managing Your Appointments

**My Appointments** (Appointments tab, or the Home tile) is where every booking across every shop and provider lives — upcoming and past, in one place.

**What you can do:**
- See the live **status** of each booking (pending, accepted, declined, completed…)
- **Cancel** or **reschedule** an upcoming one
- **Book again** with one tap for something you've booked before

**Payment on an appointment** follows the same claim/confirm system as everywhere else — if payment is due, you'll see a Pay button right on the appointment card; tap "I have paid" once you've sent it, and the seller confirms on their end. See **"Paying Safely on STRYT"** for the full picture.

**Rejected or unpaid?** If a payment claim was rejected or is still outstanding, you'll see a clear Retry option right there — no need to hunt around for how to fix it.
